Move with Left and Righg Arrows
To cross a wall you have to match the shape or the color on the rail
Oncoming shapes may match yours or not, they may repeat on more than on rail, so theres a chance that all the rails match your current shape :3
Oncoming shapes may match your color or not, if they do, they only appear once, as they are easier to recognize
If you are unable to match color or shape you are dead!
